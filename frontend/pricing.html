Frequently Asked Questions

What's the difference between quality tiers?

Free tier provides basic JSON structure, Starter offers enhanced detail with moderate NLP, and Premium delivers maximum detail with advanced NLP techniques for comprehensive content generation.

How long do paid plans last?

Each paid plan (Starter/Premium) lasts for exactly 1 month from the date of purchase. After expiry, you'll return to the free plan.

What happens if I exceed my daily limit?

You'll need to wait until the next day for your limit to reset, or purchase a plan with higher daily limits.

Is there a free plan?

Yes, the Free plan comes with 3 prompts per day, forever. No credit card required to start.

How does payment work?

All plans are one-time payments processed securely through Razorpay. No recurring charges - you only pay when you want to extend your access.

Can I upgrade my plan later?

Yes, you can upgrade to a higher tier at any time. The new plan will start immediately and last for 1 month from the purchase date.
